Embark on a Journey of Resilience and Inclusion with Love Lift Thrive


Join us for a 3-day event in collaboration with the Oakland LGBTQ Community Center. Support the LGBTQ+ community facing adversity from December 8th to 10th in Oakland. Celebrate resilience and contribute to essential services. Be part of something extraordinary







What We Are:

Love Lift Thrive is not just an event; it's a commitment to uplifting lives. We're a beacon of support for those facing adversity, especially homelessness and financial instability. Our multifaceted approach encompasses crucial wellness services, mental health support, and a dedication to inclusivity.




What We Do:

Step into a world where basic needs become accessible. Love Lift Thrive offers STD/STI screening, preventative medications, and safer sex resources. We're not just about surviving; we're about thriving. Mental health support is woven into the fabric of our project, providing a cornerstone for the low-income LGBTQ+ community.






What's In It For You:

By joining Love Lift Thrive, you gain more than an event experience. You become part of a community that stands against judgment and believes that everyone, regardless of circumstances, deserves essential resources and mental health support. Mark your calendar for December 8th-10th and join us in the vibrant heart of Oakland. This is your chance to celebrate resilience, connect with a supportive community, and thrive with us.
