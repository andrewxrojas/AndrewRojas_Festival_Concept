About Us


In collaboration with the Oakland LGBTQ Community Center, Love Lift Thrive is where the heartbeat of compassion meets the rhythm of resilience. Our transformative initiative transcends the ordinary, resonating with the very soul of the LGBTQ+ community. More than just an event, Love Lift Thrive is a commitment—an unwavering pledge to lift our community to new heights.

At the core of this mission is a dedication to those facing adversity, particularly individuals grappling with homelessness and financial instability. Love Lift Thrive is a beacon of support, fostering inclusivity and providing essential services that extend beyond the confines of conventional aid.

For those in the LGBTQ+ community navigating circumstances where basic needs become unaffordable or inaccessible, Love Lift Thrive is the answer. Our multifaceted approach encompasses crucial wellness services, from STD/STI screening and preventative medications to safer sex resources. Mental health support, a cornerstone for the low-income LGBTQ+ community, is intricately woven into the fabric of this project.

What sets Love Lift Thrive apart is our unwavering dedication to inclusivity and empowerment. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to essential resources and mental health support. This event is not just a celebration; it's a stand against judgment and a testament to the strength of a united community.

Join us in the vibrant heart of Oakland on December 8th, 9th, and 10th, as Love Lift Thrive becomes a catalyst for change. These dates mark a season of empowerment, aligning with the festive spirit while addressing critical needs. Be a part of this journey, where Love Lift Thrive transforms not only lives but the very fabric of our community—a place where everyone can not only survive but thrive.
